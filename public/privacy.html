Privacy policy

Personal information

  1. Bar Assistant actively tries to collect as little personal information data as possible.
  2. The only personal information that we store is an email address that is needed to create an account.
  3. You can change your personal information at any time via profile page.
  4. You can delete your account at any time via profile page and all your personal information will be removed.

Non-personal information

  1. When you visit the Bar Assistant we might store some information such as your device's IP address.
  2. We do not store any cookies on your device.

Purchases

  1. All purchases made through Bar Assistant are processed by a third-party payment processor, Paddle (paddle.com).
  2. We do not store any payment information like credit card information.
  3. You can view Paddle's privacy policy here.
