Main features

Recipes

Create and manage your archive of cocktail recipes. You can add personal notes, favorites, rate and add images.

Organize

Group recipes into custom collections. Find new recipes with quick and powerful filtering via tags, glasses, ratings, ingredients and more.

Importing

Found something you like? You can import recipes from other websites and Bar Assistant will do its best to extract and match data.

Sharing

Want to share a custom recipe that impressed your guests? You can print, export and generate recipe images in a few clicks.

Shelf

Shelf is your personal dashboard. Here you can see all the recipes you can make with ingredients that you have, see your shopping list, check your bar stats and more.

Bars

Manage multiple bar instances from one application. Each bar has its own recipes, ingredients, settings, shelf and users.

API

Are you an engineer? With Bar Assistant you have access to powerful API. Build your client applications.

Open Source

Complete code for API and web client is open source and MIT licensed. You can self-host your own instance and have full control of your own infrastructure.

Made in EU

Proudly made in Croatia. We collect the minimum required data needed to run and the service is GDPR compliant.

Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

Our payments are processed by Paddle. They support all major cards, PayPal, Google Pay and others.

Can I use Bar Assistant for free?

Some features that are only available to customers with the "Mixologist" plan, but most of the features of the Bar Assistant that you need for a personal use are free.

Do you offer a trial for a "Mixologist" plan?

Currently, we do not offer a trial for the "Mixologist" plan. If you have any questions about specific features please send us an email.

How do I cancel my subscription?

When you sign in to your account, go to your profile and under the "Billing" tab you will have the option to manage your plan. Once you cancel your plan, you will have access to your current plan until the end of your billing period.

What happens if I cancel my plan?

You will be downgraded to a free account. You will have an option to keep one bar activated, while the rest will be deactivated. All data for deactivated bars will be retained for one year. If you reactivate the "Mixologist" plan you can continue where you left off.
